## FORM SETTINGS

1. **Responses tab** → Collect email addresses: OFF
2. **Settings** → Make this a quiz: OFF
3. **Settings** → Limit to 1 response: OFF (allow multiple)
4. **Presentation** → Show progress bar: ON
5. **Presentation** → Confirmation message:
```
Thanks! Your feedback helps us improve.

Questions? Visit: github.com/NextShiftConsulting/swarm-it-adk/discussions
```
